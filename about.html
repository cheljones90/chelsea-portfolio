About Me

Designer. Advocate. Human first.

8 years of B2B and fintech design, shaped by a belief that good experiences start with genuinely caring about the people on the other side of the screen.

01

What Drives Me

I got into design to be an advocate. That hasn't changed.

Somewhere along the way, shipping fast became the goal, and the user got left behind. I wanted to push back on that. To be the person in the room who asks "but what does this person actually need?" before anything gets built.

That instinct carries into how I work with people too. In agencies, designers can feel invisible. Heads down, executing, rarely asked how they're doing. I've always made it a point to change that wherever I am. Checking in on colleagues, creating space for honest conversation, building the kind of culture where people feel safe to think out loud.

"My job is to advocate — for users, for the people around me, and for experiences that actually respect the humans they're built for."
