How I Work

01

Research before rectangles

Every project starts with understanding the user's actual workflow — not assumptions. I conduct interviews, synthesize findings, and map the competitive landscape before a single screen is drawn.

02

Design for the system, not the screen

Individual flows exist within larger systems. I think about how features interact, how patterns scale, and how the experience holds together across the entire product — not just the feature in front of me.

03

Advocate, then collaborate

Good design requires push and pull with engineering and product. I advocate for user needs, push back when constraints would compromise the experience, and find solutions that work for everyone.
